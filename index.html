About IEEE-SSIT

IEEE Society on Social Implications of Technology is concerned with how technology impacts the world, and how its applications can change the world for better. The VIT bureau of IEEE-SSIT was formed on January 10th, 2017 and since then we have taken up quality projects in various streams of engineering, organized multiple workshops, hosted innumerable events and left an indelible mark in the competitive landscape.
